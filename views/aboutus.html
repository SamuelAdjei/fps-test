ABOUT US

FPS is dedicated to providing the best possible solutions for private staffing in every aspect of your home and lifestyle. Of course, we are experts in recruiting elite private staff for your home, but it is just as important to us to ensure your wellbeing is nurtured by matching you with conscientious, skilled lifestyle staff, too. As a specialist consultancy arm of the trusted Fuse Luxury Group that has been providing global services to HNW and UHNW individuals for years, we take pride in making sure we work hard in finding the right candidates.


Trust Fuse

Our trusted team has over 15 years of recruitment experience. More than 85% of our business comes from referrals, proof that our reputation for providing a high level of service, care and trust is prized by our clients. If we don't have the right candidate for you on our books, we will actively headhunt on your behalf until we find your perfect match. From nannies to chefs, FPS sources the candidate that is the best fit for your household, based on your requirements and their skill set and personality.


Personal Expertise, Perfect Matches

The Fuse Private Staffing team know what our clients require from their household staff, having worked in private households as nannies, maternity nurses and housekeepers ourselves. We are particular about who we represent, both from a client and candidate perspective, as we want to ensure roles are long-lasting and relationships enduring. Let us help you to build your elite team of private and household staff to keep every area of your life running like clockwork.
